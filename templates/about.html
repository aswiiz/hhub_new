About Health Care Hub

Health Care Hub is a modern, privacy-focused application designed to help individuals track their daily health and perform quick symptom assessments.

Our Mission

To provide a simple, intuitive, and beautiful interface for health tracking without the clutter of complex medical jargon or intimidating health scores.

Key Features

  • Digital Diary: Log your mood and symptoms daily.
  • Symptom Analysis: Get a quick overview of your health status based on common symptoms.
  • Privacy First: Your data is stored locally and used only for your awareness.

Disclaimer: This application provides general information and is not a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always seek the advice of your physician or other qualified health provider with any questions you may have regarding a medical condition.
